-What actually caused the cutoff, and what changed:

The bug: sendContent_P streams raw PROGMEM chunks with no declared Content-Length (relies on HTTP chunked-transfer framing). On an ESP32 softAP, that's fragile — weak signal, a browser prefetch, or the OS's captive-portal probe hitting / mid-stream can truncate it, and you get exactly the "page cut off" symptom.

The fix: handleRoot() now builds the whole page into one String (FPSTR(PAGE_TMPL) + six .replace() calls for SSID/thresholds/push interval/firmware version), then calls srv.send(200, "text/html", page) once. WebServer::send() computes a real Content-Length from the String's length, so the browser knows exactly how many bytes to expect — nothing can be truncated mid-transfer.
